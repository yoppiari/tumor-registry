# Product Brief: inamsos

**Date:** 2025-11-17
**Author:** Yoppi
**Context:** Enterprise Healthcare Research System

---

## Executive Summary

INAMSOS (Indonesia National Cancer Database System) adalah centralized tumor database yang akan mentransformasi kolegium kemampuan penelitian dari fragmented data menjadi integrated real-time intelligence. Sistem ini tidak hanya mengumpulkan data, tapi memberikan actionable insights untuk research advancement dan predictive analytics yang membantu kolegium memahami cancer patterns di seluruh Indonesia.

---

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Peneliti kolegium saat ini menghadapi分散的 (scattered) tumor data yang tersebar di multiple center tanpa centralized intelligence. Data collection terjadi secara silo, making it impossible untuk:
- Melihat real-time cancer distribution patterns nationwide
- Mengidentifikasi emerging cancer trends secara early warning
- Melakukan cross-center research dengan adequate sample sizes
- Membuat predictive insights untuk research planning
- Memahami geographic cancer distribution patterns

### Problem Impact

Keterbatasan data intelligence ini menghambat kolegium dalam:
1. **Research Quality:** Limited sample sizes dan geographic coverage
2. **Publication Impact:** Tidak bisa publish large-scale studies
3. **Policy Influence:** Tidak memiliki data untuk evidence-based recommendations
4. **Early Detection:** Missing opportunities untuk identify cancer trend patterns
5. **Global Recognition:** Limited contribution ke international cancer databases

### Proposed Solution

INAMSOS mengatasi ini dengan centralized tumor database yang:
- **Aggregates multi-center data** secara real-time dengan standardized protocols
- **Provides geographic intelligence** untuk cancer distribution mapping
- **Enables cross-center research** dengan unified data access protocols
- **Delivers predictive analytics** untuk emerging cancer trends
- **Supports research publication** dengan comprehensive, credible data sources

### Key Differentiators

1. **Kolegium-Owned:** Internal system dengan privacy dan ownership yang terjamin
2. **Real-Time Intelligence:** Live data aggregation dengan immediate insights availability
3. **Geographic Analytics:** Spatial distribution analysis dengan actionable insights
4. **Research-Focused:** Designed specifically untuk academic research needs
5. **Predictive Capabilities:** Early warning system untuk emerging cancer patterns

## Target Users

### Primary Users

**Peneliti Kolegium** (Cancer Researchers)
- **Current Behavior:** Manual data collection dari multiple centers, limited access to comprehensive datasets
- **Frustrations:** Time-intensive data gathering, small sample sizes, inability to see national patterns
- **What They Value:** Access to large-scale, credible, real-time data untuk powerful research
- **Technical Comfort:** Moderate - comfortable with research tools but need user-friendly data access

**Kolegium Leadership** (Strategic Decision Makers)
- **Current Behavior:** Making decisions based on limited, outdated cancer statistics
- **Frustrations:** Cannot see emerging trends, cannot predict resource needs
- **What They Value:** Strategic intelligence untuk policy decisions dan resource planning
- **Technical Comfort:** Low - Need dashboards dan actionable insights, not raw data

### Secondary Users

**Center Data Entry Staff**
- **Current Behavior:** Local data entry dengan limited visibility of broader impact
- **Frustrations:** Repetitive data entry tanpa seeing research contribution value
- **What They Value:** Easy-to-use forms, understanding of research impact, quality recognition

**National Health Policymakers**
- **Current Behavior:** Rely on general health statistics tanpa cancer-specific intelligence
- **Frustrations:** Lack of specific, real-time cancer data untuk evidence-based policy
- **What They Value:** Reliable, real-time cancer intelligence untuk national health planning

### User Journey

**Researcher Journey:**
1. **Data Discovery:** Browse aggregate cancer patterns nationally → Identify research opportunities
2. **Data Request:** Apply for specific dataset access → Receive approval for research access
3. **Analysis:** Access comprehensive, clean data → Generate insights dan publications
4. **Impact:** Contribute to cancer understanding → Advance kolegium research reputation

**Leadership Journey:**
1. **Intelligence Access:** View real-time cancer dashboards → Identify emerging patterns
2. **Strategic Planning:** Use predictive insights → Plan resources dan research priorities
3. **Policy Influence:** Present data-driven recommendations → Advance national cancer care

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

**Data Collection Layer:**
- Multi-center data entry forms dengan standardized protocols
- Real-time data synchronization ke central database
- Quality validation dengan automated error checking
- Photo/document attachment capabilities

**Research Access Layer:**
- Aggregate data browsing dengan geographic visualization
- Research request workflow dengan approval system
- Tiered data access (aggregate, pseudo-anonymized, detailed)
- Collaboration tools untuk multi-center research

**Intelligence Dashboard:**
- Real-time cancer distribution maps
- Trend analysis dengan predictive indicators
- Research performance metrics
- Center comparison analytics

**User Management:**
- Role-based access control (4 role types identified)
- Center-specific data ownership controls
- Researcher verification system
- Privacy protection dengan audit trails

### Out of Scope for MVP

- Advanced AI-powered predictive analytics (future enhancement)
- Mobile applications (Phase 2)
- International data integration (future roadmap)
- Advanced genetic data analysis (specialized module)

### Future Vision

- Mobile applications untuk field data collection
- AI-powered cancer trend predictions
- Integration dengan international cancer databases
- Advanced collaboration research platform

**Phase 3 (12 months):**
- Predictive treatment outcome analytics
- Real-time clinical decision support
- International research partnerships
- Advanced data visualization tools

### MVP Success Criteria

## Risks and Assumptions

### Critical Assumptions
- Centers willing to share data dengan standardized protocols
- Researcher adoption will drive data quality improvement
- Kolegium leadership will support mandatory participation
- Technical infrastructure can handle national scale data volumes

### Key Risks and Mitigation
**Risk:** Center resistance to data sharing
**Mitigation:** Pilot success stories, recognition programs, gradual rollout strategy

**Risk:** Data quality issues from multiple entry points
**Mitigation:** Automated validation, quality incentives, training programs

**Risk:** Technical scalability challenges
**Mitigation:** Phased rollout, performance monitoring, architecture scalability planning

**Risk:** Researcher adoption slower than expected
**Mitigation:** Early researcher involvement, success case documentation, user experience optimization
